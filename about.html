About Substance

Revolutionizing skincare through personalization and expert curation

Our Mission

At Substance, we believe that skincare should be personal, not prescriptive. Every person's skin is unique, with its own specific needs, concerns, and preferences. Our mission is to simplify the overwhelming world of skincare by providing intelligent, personalized recommendations backed by in-depth product curation.

We're dedicated to helping you discover the perfect routine – one that works for your skin type, addresses your specific concerns, and fits your lifestyle. Whether you're a skincare novice or an experienced enthusiast, Substance is your trusted companion on the journey to healthier, more radiant skin.

How It Works

1

Take Our Skin Quiz

Answer 5 simple questions about your skin type, concerns, age, routine habits, and budget. Our intelligent algorithm analyzes your responses to create a comprehensive skin profile.

2

Get Personalized Recommendations

Based on your unique profile, we recommend a complete morning and evening routine with specific products curated from our expert-selected collection. Each product is chosen for maximum compatibility with your skin.

3

Shop & Transform Your Skin

Add your recommended products to your cart and check out. Follow your personalized routine with confidence, knowing each product is specifically chosen for your skin's success.
